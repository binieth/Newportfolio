How I became a software developer

In short, I was inspired by a friend. While living in CA, I had a roommate who lived and breath online almost every time. It always fascinates me how a person would sit in front of a computer for a long time for i didnt know what they would be doing. I was curious to see what he was doing. I once asked him what he was doing and he showed me some of the websites and web applications he has built. I was hooked since then. In the past few years, I have built and run a handful of WordPress e-commerce websites, blog and chrome extensions. A few years ago I moved to Columbus, I needed to challenge my self, I was interested to improve my skills and learn backend web development. I once heard an add on NPR while driving about We Can Code It software Bootcamp. I thought about it for a while, went to their office to learn more and I hopped on along with other student and I love every moment of it.
